# To study and perform PC to PC communication using ethernet

Select 2 PC Connect them through (Copper Cross Wire) Fastethernet
Double click PC0
Configure-> FastEthernet: Ip4V Address-192.168.23.1
Double click PC1
Configure-> FastEthernet: Ip4V Address-192.168.23.2
Double click PC0
Desktop-> Command Prompt: ping 192.168.23.2
Double click PC1
Desktop-> Command Prompt: ping 192.168.23.1
Send a PDU message check with stimulation
