# To create Star topology using hub and switch

5PC and 1 2960 Switch(Miscellaneous) Connect with Copper wire in Fastethernet (Auto)
6PC and 1 PT-HUB (Network Devices-> Hubs) Connect with Copper wire in Fastethernet (Auto)
Assign IPv4 Address to each of the PCs
Start the stimulation and observe the working of switch and Hub
